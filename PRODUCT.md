# HabitQuest Product Brief

HabitQuest is a game-first arcade-fantasy habit RPG.

Core direction:
- Keep the experience energetic, rewarding, and slightly theatrical.
- Treat logging, quests, bosses, gear, loot, and streaks as the primary loop.
- Prefer clear game HUD language over generic productivity language.
- Preserve fast, reliable journaling and multi-entry fallback behavior.

Product priorities:
- Sharp production builds.
- Accurate rewards, streak protection, and deletion reversal.
- Delightful but readable UI with strong mobile behavior.
- Accessible motion with reduced-motion support.

The app should feel like a playable character sheet, not a calm checklist.
